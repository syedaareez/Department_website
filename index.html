WHO WE ARE

Industrial and production engineering (IPE) is an interdisciplinary engineering discipline that includes manufacturing technology, engineering sciences, management science, and optimization of complex processes, systems, or organizations.

OUR VISION

The Department of Industrial and Production Engineering visualizes to ease human life by the way of designing integrated systems and high end techniques in the areas of Industrial, Production and Manufacturing Technology through effective teaching learning processes at the post graduate and undergraduate level and inculcate the research acumen among the students.

OUR MISSION

To impart world class knowledge in the areas of Industrial, Production and Manufacturing Technology by stimulating research and innovation. To make the students productive, flexible and customer oriented so that they can apply engineering skills to design effective systems and procedures in order to make our country competitive.

OUR EXPERTISE

Data Analytics

Be it analyzing an operation, predicting inventory for supply chain, quality assurance or planning a manufacturing strategy, Industrial Engineers develop critical thinking and insights for data gathering, monitoring, analysing and predective, which makes them rock strong in Data Analytics. Modern languages and tools like Python, R and Minitab are taught for the implementation of their skils.

Operations Management

Operations are the heart of any production process and business, be it goods or service industry. Operations management helps Industrial Engineers develop analytical for understanging the process and dwells in them deep-rooted logical skills for formulation, drafting and execution of strategies.

Statistics & Probability

Statistics and probability play a major role in real-life large scale projects and Industrial Engineers are well-versed with them as they study both the concepts and more importantly their applications across the semesters in engineering disciplines like Quality Control, Maintenance and Reliability Engineering, Supply Chain Managent etc.

Manufacturing Industry

The added Mechanical Engineering subjects make the students best suited for the manufacturing industry. Having sound knowledge of production processes, an Industrial and Production Engineer can make better sense during procurement, managing man-machine operations and materials, and hence make better strategic decisions in the manufacturing industry with the help of subjects like manufacturing technology,computer-aided manufacturing, theory of production process, theory of machines and design of machine elements.

Supply Chain Management

Supply Chain Management forms an integral part of the Industrial and Production Engineering. The management and organization of flow of raw materials, work-in-process, inventory control, as well as end to end order fulfillment from point of origin to point of consumption of both goods and services are taught in the subjects of supply chain management, materials management and project management. With the knowlede of supply chain management, Industrial and Production Engineers gets the complete overview of each stage of manufacturing of any product, from raw material to finished product and its delivery to the customer, thus they can apply their optimization techniques in supply chain as well.

Total Quality Control

Industrial and Production Engineers deeply study the systems and methods of Statistical Quality Control and Statistical Process Control under the umbrella of Total Quality Control, for bringing conformance to requirements of either goods or services. They bridge the gaps between the requirements and the services(or goods) provided by deciphering errors and inconsistent systems and further develop out-of-control-action plans to improve the processes by use of subjects like Work Study Ergonomics, Production Planning and Control etc.
