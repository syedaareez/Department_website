B.Tech in Industrial and Production Engineering



Programme Educational Objectives (PEO)


To serve the nation by producing qualified and skilled engineers who will:

  1. Excel in professional career and/or higher education by acquiring knowledge in Engineering, Computational and Statistical principles.
  2. Have the ability to analyze real life problems and develop solutions that are technically sound, economically feasible and socially acceptable.
  3. Exhibit professionalism, ethical attitude, communication skills, team work in their profession and adapt to current trends by engaging in lifelong learning.


Programme Outcomes (PO)


A student after acquiring B. Tech. in Industrial and Production engineering is expected to possess the following capabilities:

  1. An ability to apply knowledge of Mathematics, Science, Engineering and Management.
  2. Shall be able to design the systems through analytical and creative thinking.
  3. Shall be able to improve the performance of the routine activities in the system and new projects within realistic time and financial constraints.
  4. Shall be able to identify, analyze and solve real life problems.
  5. Shall have sufficient knowledge and competence to effectively make use of modern scientific tools in research and higher education.
  6. Shall be able to communicate and lead teams towards effective governance in various organizations at all levels.
  7. Shall have strong ethical & professional traits and be environmental friendly to achieve organizational sustainability through teamwork.
