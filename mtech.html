M.Tech in Manufacturing Technology



Programme Educational Objectives (PEO)


To serve the nation by producing qualified and skilled engineers who will:

  1. The graduates should be able to establish themselves as practising professional by acquiring knowledge in the field of production & Industrial Management and other related areas.
  2. The graduates should be able to develop an ability to analyze, understand, design & provide novel engineering solutions.
  3. The graduates should be able to communicate effectively and imbibe professional attitude with multidisciplinary approach towards addressing environmental & social issues.


Programme Outcomes (PO)


POs describe what students are expected to know or be able to do by the time of post-graduation from the program.

  1. Acquire knowledge of core Production & Industrial Engineering systems.
  2. Be able to analyze complex engineering problems & provide feasible solutions.
  3. Develop an ability to design and conduct experiments as well as analyze and interpret data.
  4. Develop an ability to apply latest technical knowhow & managerial approaches for enhancing productivity of organizations.
  5. Develop an ability to carry out methodical research for finding appropriate solutions & logical conclusions to further undertake research in higher education.
  6. Develop an ability to function effectively both individually as well as in teams for accomplish common goals.
  7. Develop an understanding of professional & ethical, responsibility towards environmental, societal & work related issues.
